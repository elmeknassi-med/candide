A Community Built on Knowledge & Values

Founded in 1998, Candide School has grown from a small neighbourhood institution into one of the region's most respected educational establishments. Our holistic approach balances rigorous academics with the arts, sports, and social responsibility.

We believe every child deserves a personalised learning journey. Our dedicated staff foster curiosity, critical thinking, and a love of lifelong learning in a safe and welcoming campus.

  • International curriculum aligned to modern standards
  • Small class sizes for personalised attention
  • State-of-the-art science and technology labs
  • Vibrant arts, music, and sports programmes
  • Active parent and community partnership
